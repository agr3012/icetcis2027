Keynote Speakers

Speaker 1

Dr. Eng. Zdzisław Polkowski

Professor, University Jana Wyżykowskiego, Poland

Dr. Polkowski's research covers management information systems, enterprise IT governance, and applications of artificial intelligence in organisational decision-making. He has authored and co-authored numerous papers in international journals and conference proceedings, and serves on the ICETCIS 2027 Steering Committee. Talk title and abstract will be published closer to the conference.
