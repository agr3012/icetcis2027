Registration & Fee Structure

Registration opens only after acceptance. Authors of accepted papers will be invited to register and pay through a secure registration portal expected to open in December 2026, alongside acceptance notifications. Fees below are indicative and subject to final confirmation by the Finance Committee.

Domestic Author Registration (India)

Applicable to each accepted paper requiring at least one author registration for inclusion in the proceedings. Early Bird − 10% Off rates apply to registrations completed before the early bird deadline (to be announced with the registration portal).

CategoryEarly Bird FeeRegular FeeNotes
Regular Author (1st paper) ₹9,000 ₹10,000 GST applicable extra, as per prevailing rate
Student Author Concessional — to be notified Concessional — to be notified Valid student ID / bonafide certificate required; final amount to be confirmed before registration opens
Each Additional Paper (same author / group) 10% discount on the base fee Applied on the 2nd and subsequent papers by the same author or group
GST As applicable Charged separately over and above the registration fee, per prevailing government rate

International Author Registration

For authors registering from outside India, fees are payable in US Dollars.

CategoryEarly Bird FeeRegular FeeNotes
International Author (1st paper) USD 270 USD 300 Bank/transaction charges, if any, to be borne by the remitter
Each Additional Paper (same author / group) 10% discount on the base fee Applied on the 2nd and subsequent papers by the same author or group
What the fee covers: Inclusion of one accepted paper in the proceedings (subject to Springer approval), conference kit, certificate, and access to all technical sessions across both days. Detailed inclusions will be published with the registration portal.
Phase 2 — Coming Later

Payment Portal & Paper Status

In the current phase, this website serves as the public information and Call for Papers portal. A secure registration and payment gateway, along with an author dashboard for checking paper status, allotted venue, and presentation timing, is planned for Phase 2 once papers are accepted (around January 2027). Until then, all submission and review activity is managed through Microsoft CMT, and registration fee collection will be communicated by email to corresponding authors of accepted papers.
