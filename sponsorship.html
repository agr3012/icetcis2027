Call for Sponsors

Sponsorship tiers below are indicative and subject to final approval by the competent authority of Rashtriya Raksha University. Sponsorship MoUs will be signed only after this approval is in place. To express interest now, write to icetcis2027@rru.ac.in.
Why Sponsor

Reach a Focused Research & Industry Audience

ICETCIS 2027 brings together academic researchers, government cyber agencies, and industry R&D teams working across AI, cloud, and cyber defence. Sponsorship offers visibility with this audience through the conference website, delegate kit, onsite signage, and proceedings front matter, along with direct networking opportunities with speakers, committee members, and delegates.
